Gluten Free Baked Brazilian "Salgados"

Description

The baked brazilian "salgados" made by Marta Midori are a sucess on Youtube! Her channel, focused on disseminate her gluten free recipes, it is one of few with acessible and affordable recipes that collect many great reviews in the comments section.

Brazilian "salgados" area baked soft pastry with diverse fillings, like ham and cheese, hotdogs and minced meat. This tasty snacks are really cheat to get and are everywhere on the streets, but they are commonly made with plain flour, which makes it impossible for celiacs to enjoy them. Thanks to Marta celiacs have an incredible option to make at home.

Ingredients

    200g of rice flour
    90g of tapioca starch
    50g of corn starch
    40g of sugar
    5g of xantham gum
    7g of baking powder
    10g dry yeast
    1/2 teaspoon of salt
    2 eggs
    50ml of cooking oil
    200ml of water

Steps

    Mx the dry ingredients
    Put the Oil and the egg first, and lastly the water
    Beat it until smooth (you can adjust the consistency with rice flour to get the more soft dough)
    Spreed flour on the table or cutting board
    Open the dough with the rolling pin covered in oil (cover your hands in oil too)
    Cut it in the desired formats, fill it and close it, but be careful with this steps cause the dough it's TOO FRAGILE
    Beat one egg and brush it on top
    Put on the preheated oven at 200ยบ celsius for 20 minutes or until golden
    If you want to freeze it, put in hermetic bags and freeze them still hot so they can be as soft as they came out of oven.
